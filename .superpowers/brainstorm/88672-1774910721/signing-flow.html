Create Contract

Pick template (Full-Time / Hourly / Project) → fill in company name, contact, rates → preview contract with rate card

DRAFT

Generate PDF

Contract content + rate card → formatted PDF with TechCloudPro branding, signature blocks, and terms

3

Send for Client Signature

SENT
4

Client Verifies & Signs

Client clicks link → enters 6-digit OTP sent to their email → views full contract → types or draws signature → submits
