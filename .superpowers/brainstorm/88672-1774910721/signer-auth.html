How should signers authenticate before signing?

Enterprise staffing contracts need the right balance of security and convenience

A

Email Link Only

Recipient clicks a unique secure link in the email → views contract → signs. Simplest flow. Good for most B2B staffing agreements.

Security: ⭐⭐  |  Friction: Low  |  Setup: None
B

Email Link + Access Code

You set a passcode when sending, share it separately (phone/text/Slack). Recipient enters code before viewing contract.

Security: ⭐⭐⭐  |  Friction: Medium  |  Setup: Manual coordination
C

Email Link + Email OTP RECOMMENDED

Recipient clicks link → enters 6-digit code sent to their email → views and signs. DocuSign standard for business contracts. No separate coordination needed.

Security: ⭐⭐⭐⭐  |  Friction: Low-Medium  |  Setup: Automatic
Why C? It's the DocuSign standard for most business contracts. No separate passcode coordination needed, and it creates an auditable verification trail (OTP sent → verified → signed) with timestamps.
